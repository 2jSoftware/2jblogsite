Ethical Implementation Framework

Core principles for developing responsible AI workflows that prioritize human well-being

Transparency

All AI-powered workflows should provide clear explanations of their decision-making processes, sources of information, and limitations.

  • Explainable outputs
  • Decision documentation
  • Source attribution
  • Confidence indicators

Privacy by Design

AI systems should be designed with data privacy as a foundational principle, minimizing data access and storage.

  • Schema-only processing
  • Data minimization
  • Secure processing
  • Access controls

Human Oversight

AI workflows should maintain appropriate human involvement in consequential decisions and provide override mechanisms.

  • Approval workflows
  • Confidence thresholds
  • Audit logging
  • Appeal processes

Continuous Evaluation

AI systems should be regularly monitored for performance, bias, and alignment with intended purpose.

  • Outcome metrics
  • Bias detection
  • Feedback incorporation
  • Version control
